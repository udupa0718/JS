OPERATORS

Javascript Operators are Symbols that are used to perform operations on operands.

        1. Arithmetic Operators => (Addition +, Subtraction -, Multiplication *,Division /, Modulus %, Increment ++,Decrement --)

        2. Comparison OR Relational Operators => (Is equal to ==, Identical (Equal and of same data type)===, Not equal to !=, Not Identical !==,Greater than >, Greater Equal to >=,Less Than <, Less Than or Equal to <=)

        3. Bitwise Operators => (Bitwise AND &, Bitwise OR |, Bitwise XOR ^, Bitwise NOT ~, Bitwise Left Shift <<, Bitwise Right Shift >>, Bitwise Right Shift with Zero >>>)

        4. Logical Operators => (Logical AND &, OR ||, NOT !)

        5. Assignment Opertaors => (Assign =, Add And Assign +=,Subtract And Assign -=, Multiply And Assign *=, Division And Assign /=, Modulus And Assign %=)

        6. Special or Ternary Operators => (Condition ? "true":"false";)
